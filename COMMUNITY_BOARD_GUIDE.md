## How to Use

### First Time Use - Accept Disclaimer

**Important**: Before posting your first advertisement, you must read and accept the terms and disclaimer.

1. Navigate to **Community Board** from sidebar
2. Click **"Post Advertisement"** button
3. **Disclaimer modal appears** (first time only)
4. **Read all terms carefully**:
   - Platform purpose and limitations
   - No liability clause
   - User responsibilities
   - Privacy and data protection
   - Prohibited activities
   - Safety guidelines
5. Click **"I Accept - Proceed to Post"**
6. Disclaimer acceptance is saved (won't show again)

**Note**: You can always view the full disclaimer by clicking "Read Full Terms" in the warning banner.

### Posting an Advertisement

1. Navigate to **Community Board** from sidebar
2. Click **"Post Advertisement"** button
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title*** (Required) - e.g., "Sofa Set for Sale"
   - **Type*** (Required) - For Sale / For Rent / Service Offered
   - **Category*** (Required) - Select appropriate category
   - **Description*** (Required) - Detailed description
   - **Price** (Optional) - Amount in ‚Çπ
   - **Condition** (Optional) - For items: New, Like New, Good, Fair, Used
   - **Contact Name*** (Required) - Your name
   - **Contact Mobile*** (Required) - Your phone number
   - **Contact Email** (Optional) - Your email
   - **Attachments** (Optional) - Photos or PDFs
4. Click **"Post Advertisement"**

### Adding Attachments

1. Click **"Choose File"** in attachments section
2. Select multiple photos or PDFs
3. **Limits**:
   - File types: Images (JPG, PNG, etc.) and PDFs only
   - File size: Maximum 5MB per file
   - Multiple files allowed
4. Preview appears below
5. Click **X** on any file to remove it

## Best Practices

### When Posting

1. **Clear Title**: Be specific and descriptive
2. **Detailed Description**: Include all relevant details
3. **Good Photos**: Take clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles
4. **Honest Condition**: Accurately describe item condition
5. **Fair Price**: Research market prices
6. **Complete Contact**: Provide reliable contact information
7. **Update Status**: Delete post once sold/rented

### When Buying/Renting

1. **Inspect First**: Always check item before buying
2. **Ask Questions**: Clarify doubts with seller
3. **Negotiate Politely**: Be respectful in price discussions
4. **Meet Safely**: Meet in common areas if possible
5. **Payment**: Agree on payment method beforehand
6. **Get Receipt**: For valuable items, get written receipt

### For Services

1. **Credentials**: Mention qualifications/experience
2. **Portfolio**: Attach PDFs with work samples
3. **References**: Mention if you've served other residents
4. **Availability**: Clearly state your availability
5. **Pricing**: Be transparent about charges
6. **Trial**: Offer trial session if applicable

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't upload photos

**Solutions:**
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Compress large images
- Ensure file is image format (JPG, PNG, etc.)
- Try uploading one at a time
- Clear browser cache

**Solutions:**
- Refresh the page

**Solutions:**
- Ensure you're the post creator
- Check if you're logged in

## FAQ

**Q: Who can post on Community Board?**  
A: All residents of the society can post advertisements.

**Q: Is there a limit on number of posts?**  
A: No limit, but avoid duplicate posts for same item.

**Q: Can I post commercial business ads?**  
A: Personal services are allowed. Large-scale commercial ads should be discussed with admin.

**Q: How do I mark an item as sold?**  
A: Delete the post once item is sold or rented.

**Q: Can I edit price after posting?**  
A: Yes, click Edit button on your post to update any details.

**Q: What if someone doesn't respond?**  
A: Try contacting at different times. If no response, look for other posts.

**Q: Can I post items from outside society?**  
A: Board is for society members only. Items should be relevant to community.

**Q: How long do posts stay active?**  
A: Posts stay until you delete them. Delete once item is sold/rented.

**Q: Can I share post outside society?**  
A: Posts are for society members. Sharing outside is not recommended.

**Q: What if I receive spam/scam messages?**  
A: Report to society administrator immediately.
