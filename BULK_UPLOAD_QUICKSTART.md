# Bulk Upload - Quick Start Guide

## ğŸš€ Get Started in 3 Minutes

### For Residents Upload

1. **Open Residents Page**
   - Login as Administrator
   - Click "Residents" in sidebar

2. **Download Template**
   - Click "Bulk Upload" button (top-right)
   - Select "Residents" card
   - Click "Download Template"

3. **Fill Excel File**
   ```
   Name*         | Email*              | Mobile*     | Login Name* | Password*   | Flat Number*
   John Doe      | john@example.com    | 9876543210  | john.doe    | pass123     | A-101
   Jane Smith    | jane@example.com    | 9876543211  | jane.smith  | pass123     | B-202
   ```
   - Replace sample data with your residents
   - Keep the header row (first row)
   - Fill all columns marked with *

4. **Upload File**
   - Click "Choose Excel File"
   - Select your filled file
   - Click "Upload & Process"
   - Wait for results

5. **Done!**
   - Check success count
   - Fix any errors if needed
   - Click "Done"
